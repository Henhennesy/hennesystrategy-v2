# Services

## How I help

- **Product and formulation strategy:** Clarify product concepts, use cases, formulation direction, differentiation, and innovation priorities.
- **Research and evidence strategy:** Shape smarter evidence plans, research priorities, study concepts, and scientific narratives.
- **Regulatory and policy-informed planning:** Support decision-making in evolving regulatory environments, with an eye toward both risk and opportunity.
- **Commercialization and market-entry support:** Help translate technical or scientific ideas into credible go-to-market and implementation strategies.
- **Scientific diligence and partnership support:** Provide external perspective on technologies, product claims, partnerships, and emerging strategic opportunities.
