Services

Strategic support where science, product, regulation, and execution meet

Hennesy Strategy supports organizations working where science, product strategy, regulation, and market execution intersect. Engagements can be structured as focused projects, ongoing advisory support, speaking engagements, or strategic workshops.

01

Product Strategy and Formulation Advisory

Support for teams developing cannabinoid or psychedelic-adjacent products that need a stronger strategic foundation.

  • Concept evaluation
  • Product architecture
  • Formulation direction
  • Use-case alignment
  • Ingredient and technology considerations
  • Differentiation and portfolio thinking
  • Early-stage pressure testing of product-market logic

The goal is to help teams make better bets earlier, before time and resources are spent on the wrong concept.

02

Research and Evidence Strategy

Support for organizations that want to build a smarter, more purposeful evidence plan.

  • Identifying the right research questions
  • Prioritizing evidence generation
  • Shaping study concepts
  • Clarifying endpoints that matter
  • Aligning scientific ambition with practical application
  • Strengthening the story between evidence and real-world relevance
03

Regulatory and Policy-Informed Planning

Support for teams making strategic decisions in environments shaped by uncertainty, evolving regulation, or sensitive scientific claims.

  • Strategic interpretation of regulatory context
  • Product and positioning implications
  • Risk-aware planning
  • Evidence and claims considerations
  • Market timing and pathway thinking
  • Cross-functional guidance where science, policy, and commercialization overlap
04

Commercialization and Market-Entry Support

Support for translating scientific or technical ideas into credible real-world execution.

  • Market-entry framing
  • Strategic prioritization
  • Innovation pipeline development
  • Internal decision support
  • Sharpening the bridge between product promise and market execution
  • Helping leadership teams clarify what is practical now versus later
05

Scientific Diligence and Partnership Support

Support for investors, operators, and mission-driven organizations evaluating technologies, scientific claims, partnerships, or emerging opportunities.

  • External diligence
  • Scientific and strategic review
  • Opportunity framing
  • Questions for partners or vendors
  • Evaluation of technical narratives
  • Clear-eyed perspective on where the real strengths and gaps are
