Overview

What Hennesy Strategy does

Hennesy Strategy works at the intersection of science, product development, regulation, and commercialization. The firm is led by Mike Hennesy, a strategist and product innovator whose work has spanned cannabinoid product development, clinical research support, regulatory engagement, and industry education.

Through Hennesy Strategy, Mike works with companies, academic centers, and foundations to help bridge the gap between early-stage science and real-world execution.

Fit

Who I work with

I work with organizations facing complex questions where scientific credibility and practical execution both matter.

  • Companies developing cannabinoid or psychedelic-adjacent products
  • Academic or nonprofit groups shaping research strategy
  • Investors evaluating scientific and commercial risk
  • Leadership teams refining innovation pipelines or market-entry plans
  • Organizations looking for an experienced external advisor who can move fluidly between technical detail and strategic decision-making
Services

Product and formulation strategy

Regulatory and policy-informed planning

Scientific diligence and partnership support

Approach

Why clients work with me

Most teams do not need another generic consultant. They need someone who can understand the science, see where evidence is thin, pressure-test assumptions, and help turn a promising idea into something more credible, focused, and executable.

My background has given me a front-row seat to how emerging science becomes product strategy, how research can support or fail commercialization, and where organizations often lose clarity. I bring a blend of scientific literacy, operator experience, strategic judgment, and a strong instinct for separating signal from noise.
